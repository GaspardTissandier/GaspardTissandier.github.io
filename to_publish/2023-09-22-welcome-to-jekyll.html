Welcome to my website!

Sep 22, 2023

Welcome to my professional website. I am a postdoctoral student at Rutgers Univeristy for the academic year 2023-2024. I work mostly on the interaction of machine learning and public policy, causal evaluation of data-driven systems for public use, and predictive policing.

In a broader sense, my research interests cover the use of ML and data-driven system for public policy, the interaction between human and ML algorithm from an economic and sociological point of view.

On this website, I will publish my research as well as complementary resource for my papers, such as data and replication files. I will also publish blog posts on various subjects such as literature review on my research topic, and useful resources presentation.
